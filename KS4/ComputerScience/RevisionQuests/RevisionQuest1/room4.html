Room 4: The Hall of Many Items

You enter a vast hall filled with floating objects. Only those who understand lists may pass.

Your Task: Fix filter_large(nums) so it returns a new list containing only the numbers greater than 10.

Examples:

  • filter_large([5, 12, 3, 19]) → [12, 19]
  • filter_large([1, 2, 3]) → []
  • filter_large([11, 10, 25]) → [11, 25]
You have mastered lists. The hall reveals a new path.
