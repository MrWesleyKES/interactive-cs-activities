Turing’s Mug – Escape Room

You have been granted access to Alan Turing’s wartime office at Bletchley Park. On his desk sits a battered mug, chained to a radiator with a combination lock. Your mission is to decode the clues hidden in Turing’s notes and discover the correct 3‑digit code.

Use the navigation on the left to move between puzzles at any time. Your countdown timer has already started.

Puzzle 1: Decode the Pattern

Each pattern row contains five circles. Black = 1, White = 0. Convert each row to a 5‑bit binary number → then to decimal → then translate to letters (A=1, B=2 … Z=26).
